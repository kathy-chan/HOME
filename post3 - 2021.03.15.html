Experimenting with Radial Bar Chart

Have you picked up a new hobby during the pandemic lockdown?

One of the things I start learning during my maternity leave in the middle of the pandemic is: How to play the ukulele?

For my journey to learn data visualization, I want to start with visualizing information and data that have personal importance to me. I was excited when I found that there is data available on the percentage of times a chord is used in songs. And so I decided to try create a data viz on this topic.

What is this project about:

For beginners to the Ukulele, "What ukulele chords should I learn first" is a common question. Once you master the first few chords, you can start playing hundreds of songs using just a few simple chords on your ukulele. This interactive viz shows the most popular chords in Western Pop and Rock songs. Each line shows the percentage of times the chord appear out 1797 songs on UkeAlong.com.

What is the process:

• Gather and interpret the data from Ukealong.com (https://ukealong.com/statistics)
• Create radial bar chart (see Steps to Create Radial Bar Chart here )
• Design customized map to display chord chart on an interactive fret board
